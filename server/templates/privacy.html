Privacy Policy – Tabibi (طبيبي)

Effective Date: March 1, 2026  |  Developer: MindMatrix Limited

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Name and email address collected during registration via Google Sign-In or email/password.
  • Health queries: Symptom descriptions and medical questions you enter when using the AI assistant. These are processed to provide responses and are not shared with third parties.
  • Usage data: App interaction logs (screens visited, features used) collected anonymously to improve the app.
  • Device information: Device type and OS version for crash reporting and compatibility.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Tabibi AI health assistant service.
  • To authenticate your account and maintain your session.
  • To send important service notifications (no marketing emails without consent).
  • To fix bugs and improve app performance via anonymised crash reports.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers. We use industry-standard encryption (TLS in transit, AES-256 at rest). We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase – authentication, database, and analytics (Google Privacy Policy applies).
  • AI Model Provider – health queries are processed by an AI model to generate responses. No personally identifiable information is attached to these queries.

5. Health Disclaimer

Tabibi provides general health information only. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider for medical decisions.

6. Children's Privacy

Tabibi is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at info@tabibi.clinic. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact
